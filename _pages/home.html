I love playing the piano and teaching piano to my 2 kids, 4 and 2 years old, and enjoy seeing other children learn a new musical skill. I specialize in teaching beginners up to Grade 4 (for now but soon will teach higher grades), children and adults. I have been playing the piano since I was 4 years old, and am at the moment organizing my AMEB Grade 8 and Diploma exams. For young children I use a variety of ear-based methods to help them to learn in an enjoyable environment, whilst they are only starting to read simple things. For adults, we use the book-based methods. There are many choices of books I will recommend to personalize to your piano androgogical goals.
